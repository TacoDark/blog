INTRODUCING.... Shoot n' Grab!

Shoot n' Grab is a unity game I am going to start developing, where you well... Shoot n' Grab. This game will be a high paced fighting game where you fight npcs (and maybe people in the future), while grabbing stuff and using it to your advantage. You start off with a gun, and you can grab items to improve your gun and cause chaos..
